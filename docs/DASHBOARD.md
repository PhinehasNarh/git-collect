## How to verify a badge

1. Go to `https://github.com/PhinehasNarh`.
2. Scroll to the **Achievements** panel on the left of the profile.
3. Click a badge to see tier and the contributing activity.
4. Allow up to 24 hours after the triggering action; GitHub awards on a delay.

## Notes

- Private contributions must be counted: profile settings must allow it, and the
  Achievements display must be enabled.
- If Galaxy Brain or Starstruck stay blocked because you keep `git-collect`
  private, that is expected. Use a public companion repo for those two only.
